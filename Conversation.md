The intellectual core is that ratio metrics (CTR etc.) are weighted averages, so the honest RCA must split a move into a *rate effect* (segments genuinely changed) vs a *mix effect* (traffic shifted toward lower/higher performers) — otherwise you attribute drops to the wrong cause (Simpson's paradox).
